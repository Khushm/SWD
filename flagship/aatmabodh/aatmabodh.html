About The Event

The word 'Aatmabodh' itself spurs motivation in our minds. Self-education is the first step in changing society. Aatmabodh gave a chance to senior citizens and housewives for bridging the technological gap in generations.'Aatmabodh' is a digital literacy program carried out since 2008.Basic usage of computers, smartphones, government apps, MS office and a range of such technological education was fostered in these curious minds.
